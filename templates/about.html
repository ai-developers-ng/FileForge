About FileForge

Your all-in-one file conversion platform

FileForge is a powerful, free online tool designed to make file conversion and text extraction simple and accessible for everyone. Whether you need to extract text from scanned documents, convert images between formats, or transform audio and video files, FileForge has you covered.

Our Mission

We believe that powerful file conversion tools should be accessible to everyone, without complicated software installations or expensive subscriptions. FileForge brings professional-grade conversion capabilities directly to your browser.

OCR Technology

Extract text from scanned documents and images with high accuracy using Tesseract OCR.

Image Conversion

Support for 200+ image formats including RAW, HEIC, WebP, and professional formats.

Audio Processing

Convert between audio formats and extract audio from video files with FFmpeg.

Video Conversion

Transform videos between formats with quality control and optimization options.

Document Conversion

Convert between PDF, DOCX, HTML, Markdown, and other document formats.

Privacy First

Your files are processed securely and automatically deleted after conversion.

Technology Stack

Tesseract OCR ImageMagick FFmpeg Pandoc Python Flask Apache Tika
